ABOUT US

Krishna Packers - Your One-Stop Solution for Industrial Packaging Needs.

Krishna Packers is a leading industrial packaging solution organization that specializes in manufacturing a wide range of packaging products, including corrugated boxes, mono cartons, rigid boxes, paper carry & mailer bags, wooden pallets, and poly bags. Established in 1991, Krishna Packers has been serving its customers with top-quality packaging products and services for several years.

At Krishna Packers, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products and services. We believe that quality is of utmost importance, and hence we always prefer quality over quantity.

Our team of experts at Krishna Packers is dedicated to delivering innovative and customized packaging solutions that are designed to meet the specific needs of our customers. We have a strong Research and Development team that works relentlessly to design and develop packaging products that are tailor-made to the needs of our clients. Our fit-to-product packaging solutions have been admired and incorporated by leaders in various industries, including the automobile and solar industries.

We understand that quality is critical, and we take utmost care to ensure that our customers receive the highest quality products. We have a dedicated Quality Assurance team that ensures that all our products meet the highest quality standards. We incorporate multiple quality checks in our manufacturing process to deliver cutting-edge satisfaction to our customers.

Our product range includes a wide variety of packaging products, including corrugated boxes, mono cartons, rigid boxes, paper carry & mailer bags, wooden pallets, and poly bags. Our corrugated boxes come in different sizes and shapes to suit the specific needs of different industries. Our mono cartons are made from high-quality materials and are available in a range of designs and colors. Our rigid boxes are strong and durable, making them ideal for storing and transporting delicate products. Our paper carry & mailer bags are eco-friendly and are available in a range of sizes and designs. Our wooden pallets are sturdy and durable, making them ideal for transporting heavy goods. Finally, our poly bags are available in a range of sizes and are ideal for storing and transporting products.

In conclusion, Krishna Packers is committed to providing its customers with top-quality products and services at cost-effective prices. Our customer-centric approach, commitment to innovation, and dedication to quality have made us a trusted name in the industrial packaging industry. Whether you need customized packaging solutions or standard packaging products, Krishna Packers is your one-stop solution for all your industrial packaging needs.
